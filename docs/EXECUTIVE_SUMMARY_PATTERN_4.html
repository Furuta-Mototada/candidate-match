❌ Problem

Most Japanese voters don't know how their representatives vote. Media focuses on party labels, not legislative behavior. There is a transparency gap between citizens and their parliament.

✅ Solution

Candidate Match bridges this gap using AI. Users answer questions about real bills and get matched with Diet members who vote like them.
