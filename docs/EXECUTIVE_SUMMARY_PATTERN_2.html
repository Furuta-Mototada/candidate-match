❌ The Problem

Japan's National Diet passes hundreds of bills each session, yet most voters have little insight into how their representatives actually vote. Media focuses on party labels and personalities — not policy. Citizens cannot easily determine which politicians truly share their views.

✅ The Solution

Candidate Match asks users a short series of questions about real bills — "Support or Oppose?" — and uses Computerized Adaptive Testing and machine learning to match them with Diet members whose voting records are most similar to their own preferences.

Key Features

🤝

🎯 Impact & Vision

Candidate Match makes legislative data accessible and personal to strengthen democratic participation in Japan.

  • Help voters choose by policy, not party label
  • Expand to local assemblies
  • Support civic education for young voters
  • Promote transparency in government
