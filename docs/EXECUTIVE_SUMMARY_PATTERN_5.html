❌ The Problem

Japan's National Diet passes hundreds of bills each session, yet most voters have no insight into how their representatives actually vote. Media focuses on party labels and personalities — not policy. Citizens cannot easily find politicians who share their views.

✅ The Solution

Candidate Match uses Computerized Adaptive Testing (CAT) and machine learning. Users answer questions about real bills — "Support or Oppose?" — and get matched with Diet members whose voting records are most similar to their own preferences.

🚀 Impact & Vision

By making legislative data accessible and personal, Candidate Match strengthens democratic participation in Japan. The vision: expand to local assemblies, support civic education for young voters, and help everyone engage with politics based on policy, not party labels.
